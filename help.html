Get Started with Shadow Intern

Set up in 30 seconds

1 Get a plan at shadowintern.xyz
Visit shadowintern.xyz/pricing to choose a plan that fits your needs.
2 Copy your license key
After purchasing, you'll receive a license key. Copy it from your account dashboard or email.
3 Paste in Settings
Open Shadow Intern settings (click the extension icon → "Full settings"), paste your license key in the License tab, and click "Check status".
